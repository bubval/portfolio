Evolutionary AI Algorithm for Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a side-scroller where the player controls a bird, attempting to fly between columns of green pipes without hitting them. The game was developed by dotGears and released in May 2013. This project successfully implements an AI that teaches itself how to play the game to the point of being perfect. Essentially, after a couple of generations the AI becomes exponentially better until it eventually reaches a point where it cannot lose. The project uses a generic algorithm, specifically NEAT – Neuroevolution of augmenting topologies.
